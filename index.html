Special Pets Types

Sphynx cat the Sphynx hairless body feels like a soft peach to the touch. While their wrinkled mug may express worry or apprehension, these cats are actually clownish comedians who love being the center of attention. The Sphynx is an affectionate, intelligent, and energetic feline who, according to Cat-World, has been described as “part cat, part dog, part monkey, and part child.”

Afghan Hound Dog It is a hound distinguished by its thick, fine, silky coat, and a tail with a ring curl at the end. The breed is selectively bred for its unique features in the cold mountains of Afghanistan. Its local name is Tāžī Spay or Sag-e Tāzī

Green Turtle Green sea turtles have many other names like a black sea turtle, pacific green turtle, and simply green turtle.
